===Procedure for METIS_img_n_cal_standard ===
 
Imaging observations of standard stars with a purpose to determine the zero point of the
photometry. The observing template is identical to the ''METIS_img_n_obs_GenericChopNod'' template, and it is combined with ''METIS_img_n_acq'' that is used to acquire the standard star.

===Parameters for METIS_img_n_cal_standard ===

^Parameter             ^Hidden     ^Range (default)            ^Label^  
|DPR CATG              |yes        |CALIB TEST (CALIB) |Data product category|
|DPR TYPE              |yes        |STD (STD)|Data product type|                      

All other parameters are identical to ''METIS_img_n_obs_GenericChopNod''
