===Procedure for METIS_img_n_cal_psf ===
 
A template used for imaging PSF calibrator stars, identical to the corresponding photometric standard star template. It is combined with ''METIS_img_n_acq'' that is used to acquire the psf calibrator star.

===Parameters for METIS_img_n_cal_psf ===

The template keywords are identical to the ''METIS_img_n_cal_standard'' template.
